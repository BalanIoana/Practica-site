Summer Sale

The campaign takes place between 28.06.2020, 08:00 - 10.07.2020, 13:30, for online shopping on the iDesign & Deco website, within the available stock, in Romania.
The object of the Campaign is any product sold online on iDesign & Deco, available for sale during the announced duration of the Campaign and expressly signaled with a discount of up to 60% compared to the price recommended by the supplier for the selected products.

Between 28.06.2020, time 8:00 - 10.07.2020, time 00:30, to any purchases made on the site are added loyalty points with a value 5 times higher than the initial one (5% of the value of the product / products ordered, compared to 1% - initial value).
Orders benefit from fast delivery. The discounts in the campaign can be combined with other benefits granted to iDesign & Deco customers when purchasing products from the online store. Also, when purchasing the products from the Campaign, you can use the gift voucher worth 50 RON for registration on the site.

Regulation amendant. Interruption or extension of the Campaign

1. iDesign & Deco reserves the right to modify this Regulation at any time, informing the participants by posting the changes on the site.
2. iDesign & Deco reserves the right to suspend or interrupt the Campaign at any time during its duration and to extend it, informing the participants about any suspension, interruption or extension by posting on the iDesign & Deco website.
3. Changes in the rules or validity of the Campaign will not affect orders already placed, but not yet finalized at the time of the change.
